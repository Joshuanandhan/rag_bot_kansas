## Be courteous and signal your intentions as you approach and drive through a traffic circle or roundabout:
路 If you intend to turn right, signal "right" while approaching and until you have exited the traffic circle or roundabout.
路 If you are going straight through, no signal is needed while approaching. However, signal "right" before the exit you want to take.
路 If you intend to turn left, once you have traveled three quarters of the way around the traffic circle or roundabout, signal "right" to take the exit.
